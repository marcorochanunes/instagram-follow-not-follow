Welcome to Instagram Followers Checker

Follow these steps to use the application:

  1. Go to Instagram Information and Permissions.
  2. Tap "Download or Transfer your information" → "Some of your information" → Select "Followers and following" and tap "Next".
  3. Choose the following settings:
    • Format: JSON
    • Date range: All Time
  4. Tap "Create file" and wait for the email from Instagram.
  5. Download the ZIP file from the email and extract it.
  6. Inside the extracted folder, find followers_1.json and following.json.
  7. Choose both files using the form below and click Upload.
  8. If the files are successfully uploaded, click the View Results button to see who doesn't follow you back and who you don't follow back.

Privacy Notice: This application does not access, store, or share any private data. The uploaded files only contain public follower and following lists from Instagram, without any sensitive or personal information.
